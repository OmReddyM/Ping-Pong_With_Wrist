AI Ping Pong











Developed by Om M || Inspration from Atari Inc.

Instructions

  1. In Ping Pong, you will play against a computer in a match of Pong.
  2. To move your paddle, use your right wrist.
  3. The computer will move its paddle automatically.
  4. Hit the ball past the computer's paddle to get points and don't let it get past yours!
